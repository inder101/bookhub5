The Stand

A post-apocalyptic horror novel by Stephen King, "The Stand" follows survivors of a global pandemic as they become embroiled in a battle between good and evil, with the charismatic and malevolent Randall Flagg leading the dark side.

Carrie

Stephen King's debut novel, "Carrie," tells the story of a shy, bullied high school girl who discovers she has telekinetic powers, which she ultimately uses to exact revenge on her tormentors during a disastrous prom night.
