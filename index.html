    Death Note

    "Death Note" is a manga about Light Yagami, a student who gains the power to kill with a notebook. He clashes with the detective L in a high-stakes battle of wits, exploring themes of justice and morality.

    One peice

    "One peice" is a popular manga series by Elichiro Oda that follows Monkey D.Luffy and his diverse crew as they search for the legendary treasure known as the One peice, aiming to become the Pirats King. The series is known for its rich world-building, unique characters, and epic adventures spanning countless islands and challenges.

    Bleach V1

    Bleach Volume 1 introduces Ichigo Kurosaki, a high school student with the ability to see ghosts. His life changes when he meets Rukia Kuchiki, a Soul Reaper tasked with defending the living world from evil spirits called Hollows. After Rukia is injured in battle, Ichigo inherits her powers, stepping into a world of supernatural battles and responsibilities.

    Attack on titan Volume 1 introduces Hajime Isayama, On the brink of extinction as gigantic humanoid creatures, the Titans, devour humans seemingly without reason, and Eren Yeager's world is shattered when his mother becomes a victim, fueling his determination to fight back.
