Tales from the dispatch

Tales from the dispatch v1

"Tales from the Dispatch: Volume 1" is the first collection in a comic series that chronicles the experiences of emergency dispatchers. This volume introduces readers to the high-pressure world of dispatch, filled with real-life inspired stories that blend tension, humor, and humanity, offering a glimpse into the lives of those who handle crises from the other end of the line.
